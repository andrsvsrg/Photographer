About Me

Welcome to my broad photography space, where you show all precious moments of life.

“ You only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough. ”

Mae West

I’m Sergey Fartuch, a photographer and filmmaker living in Florida. I’m happy to say that I’m one of the top 10 artists working in Florida. My way was long – film making, branding, painting and lots of other interesting things. My digital photo manipulation and compositing made it possible for me to work in different areas inducing journalism, nature photography, digital portraits and lot of other stuff.

My photography collections take part in different festivals, so I’m sure you’ll love what you see on your photos. I love to share what I do and happy to say that it’s a wonderful way to receive feedback and develop yourself. I’m also a leading photographer is fashion and travel.
